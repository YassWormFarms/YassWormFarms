Our Enhanced Worm Leachate System

We enhance our worm leachate by adding molasses, Tri Kelp Concentrate and K-Humic-26% through a 40-hour fermentation process. During this time the system is continuously circulated and aerated to maintain oxygen levels and massively multiply beneficial microbial communities.

Why Worm Leachate Works Differently

Traditional fertilisers like superphosphate and synthetic inputs add nutrients directly to soil, but much becomes locked up and unavailable to plants.
