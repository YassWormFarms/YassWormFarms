Our Enhanced Worm Leachate System

We enhance our worm leachate by adding molasses, Tri Kelp Concentrate, Omnia Fulvic Acid and K-Humic-26% through a 40-hour fermentation process. During this time the system is continuously circulated and aerated to maintain oxygen levels and massively multiply beneficial microbial communities.

When Bacteria, Yeasts and other microbes start multiplying they metabolise the sugars and produce CO2 Gas, Proteins and organic compounds called surfactants creating the foam. A good active foam indicates strong microbial growth, oxygenation and active fermentation biology.
