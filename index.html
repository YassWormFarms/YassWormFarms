Why Worm Juice Works Differently

Traditional fertilisers like superphosphate add nutrients directly to your soil, but much becomes locked up and unavailable to plants.

Worm juice contains over 500,000 microbes per gram and high fungal activity, activating soil biology to unlock existing nutrients.

Use Less Fertiliser — Not None

Many farmers cut superphosphate use by 20–40% while maintaining or improving pasture growth.

Fertiliser Prices Are Rising

Global supply disruptions and war have significantly increased fertiliser prices. Reducing reliance on imported inputs protects your farm.

The Smart Approach

Combine worm juice with reduced fertiliser:
✔ Lower costs
✔ Better soil biology
✔ Improved long-term productivity
