Why Worm Juice Works: Key Microbes

  • Pseudomonas: Helps plants tolerate lower frost temperatures
  • Sorangium: Produces natural fungicides and bactericides that outcompete pathogens
  • Fungi Penicillium: Produces enzymes, antibiotics, and growth regulators
  • Methylobacterium: Stimulates seed germination
  • Streptomyces: Provides natural antifungals, antivirals, and antibiotics
  • Myxococcus: A predator that eliminates unwanted bacteria in the soil
  • Lactobacillus: Supports plant resistance to heat, radiation, toxins, and extreme pH levels

Red Wigglers compost worms are known to produce many of these microbes – we primarily use this worm variety in our worm beds.
