Application Rates

5L – Home Gardens

Mix 1:10 with water. Apply every 2–4 weeks.

20L – Small Farms & Horticulture

Apply 20–50L per hectare depending on soil condition.

220L – Commercial Use

Use in fertigation systems or broadacre spray applications.

IBC 1000L – Large Scale Farming

Designed for regenerative agriculture and soil biology recovery programs.
