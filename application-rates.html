Our Story

Yass Worm Farms is a family-owned business producing premium worm juice and castings for farmers, gardeners and soil enthusiasts across New South Wales. Our mission is to improve soil health naturally, sustainably and efficiently.

Beginning as a small operation, we’ve grown through dedication, hard work and the support of our passionate team. Every product we create reflects our care for the land and our commitment to eco‑friendly farming.

Healthy soil leads to healthier plants and better harvests. Our philosophy is simple: nourish the soil, and it will nourish you.
