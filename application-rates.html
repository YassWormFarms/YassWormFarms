Our Story

Yass Worm Farms is a family-owned business producing premium worm juice and castings for farmers, gardeners, and soil enthusiasts across New South Wales. Our mission is to improve soil health naturally, sustainably, and efficiently.

Beginning as a small backyard operation, we’ve grown through dedication, hard work, and the support of our passionate team. Every product we create reflects our care for the land and our commitment to eco-friendly, sustainable farming practices.

We believe in the power of healthy soil — it leads to healthier plants, better harvests, and a thriving environment. Our philosophy is simple: nourish the soil, and it will nourish you.
