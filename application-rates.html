Worm Leachate Fertiliser Application Guide

Rocket fuel for plants — a living microbial fertiliser that unlocks soil nutrients, improves plant health, and reduces synthetic fertiliser costs.
✔ Always apply during active growth for best results

✔ Worm Leachate works biologically — more microbes = stronger soil response

✔ Can reduce synthetic fertiliser use over time

🌱 Pots / Containers / Seedlings

100 mL worm juice + 1 L water (1:10)

Apply as a soil drench every 1–2 weeks during active growth.

🌿 Garden Beds (Soil Activation)

100 mL + 5 L water

Drench root zones thoroughly. Repeat every 1–2 weeks.

🍃 Foliar Feed (Leaf Spray)

50–100 mL + 1 L water (1:20 to 1:10)

Spray early morning or late afternoon for best absorption.

Repeat every 1–2 weeks.

🌾 Lawns (Household Use)

50–200 mL worm juice per 50 m²

Improves colour, density, and soil biology.

🚜 Broadacre / Commercial Crops

Minimum 100 L total liquid per hectare

Recommended mix:
5–15 L worm juice per hectare diluted in water as a carrier.

Water rate is not critical — it distributes biology across the crop.

Worm Leachate has an accumulative biological effect rather than a short-term chemical spike.
✔ Higher concentration = stronger long-term soil improvement

✔ Biological systems build over time unlike synthetic fertilisers

⚠️ Precautions & Best Practice

• Do NOT use freshly chlorinated water — let stand 24 hours or use rainwater

• Once mixed, apply immediately (do not store diluted solution)

• Apply at dawn or dusk or when foliage is cool/damp

• Start with lower dilution (1:20) for sensitive plants

• Can be mixed with organic inputs such as Molasses, Kelp and Humic Acid

• If plants respond well increase frequency or concentration gradually

• For storage keep sealed, vented, cool and out of sunlight
